About ASD

The reason behind this website is to help show that people are not alone. Showing that we are all in this world together. Many people struggle with getting what is stressing them out off of their chest. It is easier to cope with stress, anxiety, and depression if you know that you are not alone. This website is to help those who don’t feel comfortable being able to come and talk to people. Too many people suffer from Anxiety, Stress, and Depression.

About the Creator

The Creator of this website has seen many people struggle with talking about their feelings. He has also gone through some of these struggles. He thought that it would help to make a website that would show people that they are not alone and that maybe talking about it, to help get it off of their chests, would help.
